# Pomodoro Timer⏱️
*A timer to measure your sessions, capturing your progress and maintaining a streak!*

# Features
- Save your timers and see how many pomodoro sessions you had today
- Sound FX everytime you finished a new session
- Popular quotes to get you motivated
- New GUI and Visuals to enhance your experience!
